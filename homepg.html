What is Galctic Conquest?

Galactic Conquest is a massively multiplayer online role-playing game inspired by sci-fi, set in a fictional galaxy. The GC universe offers many factions within which players can roleplay, or they can play independently of these factions. Faction battles across planets or exploring major cities are some of the activities available. Players to forge their own destiny by choosing to fight for one of many official player led factions for control of the Galaxy. Galactic Conquest will offer several RPG only planets designed for role play and fights against NPC monsters and factions while at the same time having a vast amount of multiplayer PvP planets which can be conquered by a faction for increased power to them. The game is currently in heavy development.
